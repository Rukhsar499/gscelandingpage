George Kids School is more than just a preschool. It is a place where young minds are nurtured, curiosity is encouraged, and a love for learning is instilled from the very beginning. Our dedicated team of educators create a safe and stimulating environment where children can explore, discover, and develop essential skills that will set them up for a lifetime of success. Our Classes

At George Kids School, we are dedicated to providing a holistic and enriching educational experience for your child. With our ENVISAGE curriculum, we strive to encourage and nurture their emotional development, while also focusing on core academics. We believe in instilling strong values and critical thinking skills, as well as fostering innovation and conflict resolution abilities. Our goal is to help shape strong character formation and attitudes that promote healthy social skills. Through our hands-on lessons, we aim to help children grow and develop good manners, all while ensuring they enjoy the learning journey. At George Kids School, we are committed to providing a well-rounded education that prepares your child for a bright future

We provide resources, tips, and guidance to help parents navigate the challenges and joys of raising a child in today's fast-paced world. From educational activities to parenting advice, Parent's Corner is here to support and empower parents in their role as the primary educators of their children.
