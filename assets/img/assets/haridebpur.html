Haridebpur Branch

George kids has pioneered curriculum development, bringing an educative experience for young ones with a thirst for knowledge. We understand that interactive education is the best way to learn and realize key concepts of life. We strive to provide every one of our lovely students with an equal level of learning. Our teachers and guardians work in unison so your child derives a well-balanced and comprehensive personality development. Our teachers keep an open line of communication with parents, helping to foster a spirit of cooperation and teamwork. Join us in our vision of a blooming and vibrant future for our country!

With more than a century of experience in educating young minds of this nation, George kids is the best preschool for parents who want their children to grow up as dignified and notable citizens. We are more than just a preschool. We are a centre for nurturing and stimulating young minds and sending them in the right direction in life. We aim to create an atmosphere where your child can feel safe and realize their true potential. We look at all our little ones as part of our family here at George kids, where learning and play go hand in hand!
